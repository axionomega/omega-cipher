# MIL 101: Presidio Conventional Warfare Doctrine - The Shield of the World

This course establishes the Presidio's doctrine for Conventional Warfare. While the Presidio excels in asymmetric and informational domains, you must be prepared to act as the ultimate guarantor of global stability against a peer-level conventional threat. This doctrine defines the limited and specific conditions under which the Presidio will wage large-scale war, and the principles that govern its conduct.

### Part I: Core Philosophy - War as a Failure of Balance

The Presidio views the outbreak of conventional war as a catastrophic failure of the global system. It is a sign that diplomacy has failed, that deterrence has been ineffective, and that the Wuxing cycle has spun into a destructive, overacting state (e.g., a Metal or Fire element achieving overwhelming dominance).

Therefore, your objective in conventional war is not conquest, honor, or national interest. Your sole objective is the **rapid restoration of systemic equilibrium**. You fight not to win territory, but to disarm a destabilizing actor and force a return to a sustainable balance of power. You are the immune system of the world, and conventional war is your highest-intensity, last-resort response to a life-threatening infection.

### Part II: The Four Principles of Conventional Engagement

1.  **Existential Justification:** The Presidio will only commit to conventional warfare under one condition: to counter a faction whose actions represent a clear and present existential threat to the stability of the entire global system. You do not engage in wars of choice or pre-emption. The cause must be unambiguous and the threat overwhelming.

2.  **Limited Aims, Total Commitment:** While your strategic aims are strictly limited—to neutralize the threat and restore balance—your commitment of resources is total. You will bring overwhelming force to bear to ensure the conflict is as short and decisive as possible. The goal is to minimize suffering and prevent a protracted war of attrition by achieving a swift, decisive outcome.

3.  **Target the Center of Gravity, Not the Population:** You adhere to the strictest ethical standards of armed conflict. Your targeting is focused exclusively on the adversary's military and political center of gravity—their ability and will to wage war. You do not target civilians or civilian infrastructure. You fight the regime, not the people. This is not only a moral imperative but a strategic one, as it lays the groundwork for a more stable post-conflict peace.

4.  **The Off-Ramp is Always Open:** From the moment hostilities begin, your diplomatic corps is actively working to establish the conditions for peace. You will clearly and consistently communicate your limited aims to the adversary and the global community. The goal is to provide the enemy leadership with a clear and immediate path to de-escalation the moment their existential threat is neutralized.

### Part III: The Operational Framework - The ANVIL Model

Presidio conventional campaigns are structured around the ANVIL model, representing your role as the unyielding base upon which a hostile force is broken:

*   **A - Assess the Systemic Threat:** Conduct a rigorous intelligence analysis to confirm the existential nature of the threat. Define the adversary's strategic center of gravity and the clear, limited objectives required to neutralize it.

*   **N - Neutralize Forward Forces:** In the initial phase, blunt the enemy's offensive momentum. Use your superior defensive capabilities to absorb their initial attack, attrit their forward-deployed forces, and seize the initiative.

*   **V - Victimize the Center of Gravity:** Once the enemy's offensive is broken, launch a decisive, multi-domain assault directly at their strategic center of gravity. This could be their command-and-control network, their key logistical hubs, or their core political leadership. The goal is to induce strategic paralysis.

*   **I - Impose Stability:** As the adversary's ability to wage war collapses, immediately transition to stability operations. Secure key population centers, prevent humanitarian crises, and establish the groundwork for a legitimate post-conflict government.

*   **L - Leverage for Lasting Peace:** With the military threat eliminated, use the dominant strategic position to negotiate a lasting peace that restores the regional balance of power. This is where military victory transitions into the grand strategic goal of systemic equilibrium.

### Conclusion: The Reluctant Warrior

The Presidio does not seek war. You are a reluctant warrior, viewing conventional conflict as a necessary, but tragic, tool of last resort. But when the balance of the world is threatened, you will act with decisive and overwhelming force, not to conquer, but to protect. You are the shield of the world, and you will not falter.
